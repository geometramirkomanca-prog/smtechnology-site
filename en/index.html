Our story

Company

SM Technology Service SRL is a company specialised in providing industrial services for the automotive and mechanical sector. We deliver treatment and quality control services for special aluminium castings supplied to major manufacturers in the automotive industry.

Our production facility is located in Monteodorisio (CH), in the Valsinello Industrial Zone, with a covered area of 1,500 m² and an uncovered area of 3,000 m², equipped with state-of-the-art technological systems.

We operate on 3 production shifts with a structured and qualified team, ensuring operational continuity and rapid response to our clients' needs.

Our services

What We Do

01

Receiving & Acceptance

Aluminium castings are received and subjected to incoming inspection with visual and dimensional checks. Each batch is tracked and logged before entering the production line.

02

Shot Blasting & Deburring

Castings undergo shot blasting for surface cleaning and deburring to eliminate residual casting burrs, ensuring proper surface preparation for subsequent treatments.

03

Vacuum Impregnation

The vacuum impregnation process seals micro-porosity in castings using acrylic resin. The cycle runs via automatic CAPI systems: vacuum, resin immersion, resin recovery, hot-water polymerisation.

04

Conformity Inspection

Every part undergoes systematic checks: visual inspection, attribute (pass/fail) and variable (dimensional measurement) controls. Conformity is verified against the client's technical specifications.

05

Rework Operations

Parts showing surface porosity not covered by impregnation are treated with acrylic resin manually. Polymerisation is carried out with a UV lamp for fast, certified results.

06

Shipping & Traceability

Conforming products are packaged and shipped to clients with full quality documentation. Batch traceability is guaranteed from receipt to delivery.

How

Surface inspection

Technical details of our impregnation systems. The two automatic CAPI systems guarantee high productivity, process repeatability and the highest quality of treatment on aluminium castings.

Impregnation System N° 1

Automatic system for vacuum impregnation of special aluminium castings. Fully automated cycle with process control.

  • Technology: Vacuum impregnation with acrylic resin
  • Cycle: Vacuum → Immersion → Resin recovery → Polymerisation
  • Polymerisation: Hot water bath
  • Application: Aluminium castings for automotive
Automatic System

Impregnation System N° 2 — CAPI

Second automatic CAPI system to guarantee production continuity and high-volume treatment capacity.

  • Brand: CAPI (certified system)
  • Technology: Automatic vacuum impregnation
  • Cycle: Vacuum → Resin immersion → Recovery → Polymerisation
  • Resin: Acrylic (for internal and surface porosity)
CAPI System — Automatic

UV Rework Station

Manual system for treating residual surface porosity using acrylic resin with UV polymerisation.

  • Method: Manual acrylic resin application
  • Polymerisation: UV lamp
  • Application: Surface porosity not treatable in automatic cycle
  • Outcome: Recovery of non-conforming parts, scrap reduction
